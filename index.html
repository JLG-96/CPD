About the club

CPD Yr Wyddgrug was founded last year by 2 very close friends; Joel Thomas and James Jones. What started as a weekly 5 a-side game between friends developed into forming a brand new club based in the Mold area. A very close group of friends form the club and make up the commitee. The club prides itself being formed upon the foundations of freindship and family. Having overachieved in their first official season by finishing 4th in the league and reaching a semi-final, the club looks to the upcoming season with ambition and excitement. With pre-season well underway, and a new ground to call home, CPD Yr Wyddgrug are itching to get this season underway.
